## Safety Considerations and Best Practices

### Product Storage and Handling

Proper kratom storage becomes especially important in San Francisco's varied climate conditions. Humidity fluctuations and temperature changes can affect product quality, making appropriate storage techniques essential for maintaining potency and preventing contamination.

**Storage best practices:**

**Airtight containers** protect kratom from moisture and air exposure that can degrade alkaloid content. Glass jars with tight-fitting lids or vacuum-sealed bags provide optimal protection for long-term storage.

**Cool, dark locations** preserve kratom potency by preventing heat and light degradation. Avoid storage areas near windows, heating vents, or appliances that generate heat.

**Moisture control** prevents mold and bacterial growth that can compromise product safety. Silica gel packets or other desiccants help maintain optimal humidity levels in storage containers.

**Proper labeling** ensures strain identification and tracks purchase dates for rotation purposes. Include vendor information, strain names, and acquisition dates on all containers.
